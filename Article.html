FRANZ LEE RAMOS

was born in Llanera, Nueva Ecija. At three months old he moved to a small town in Manila, the Philippines where was raised by his parents. After taking a slew of classes in Top Link Global College Parsons School of Photography, and the International Center for Photography, he came home to Llanera and promptly staked his claim on the city's burgeoning fashion industry, producing a formidable body of work that includes some of the era's most memorable fashion and celebrity images.

In 2019, he condensed the milestones of the first few years of his career in the book Push: Muses, Mischief, & How to Make it in Manila, which became an instant bestseller and social media sensation. Aside from being highly requested by the top local celebrities, he has also turned his lens on international personalities like Elsa Hosk, Coco Rocha, Sky Ferreira, Pietro Boselli, Noah Centineo, and Troye Sivan.
